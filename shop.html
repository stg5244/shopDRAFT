Gas Station on Mars

In "Retro Gas Station on Mars," you play as a space traveler who has landed on the red planet and needs to refuel their ship. You must navigate through the abandoned gas station, solving puzzles and overcoming obstacles, while uncovering the secrets of why the station was abandoned. With a retro-futuristic aesthetic and a gripping story, "Retro Gas Station on Mars" is a unique and exciting adventure game.
